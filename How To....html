Welcome to BarTinder.

BarTinderis a drink match-making social app. To use BarTinder properly, you'll first have to install the app and create an account. Once your account is live and you're acquainted with a simple BarTinder interface and settings, we'll be matching you with drinks in no time.
1.) Review the BarTinder drink selection page. You'll see an image in the middle of the page; this is a profile of a random drink and it's ingredients.
2.) Look at the buttons on screen. These buttons allow you to interact with random drink profiles. From left to right, these buttons do the following: Dislike - Tap the Left button to dislike a drink profile and receive another suggestion. Like - The Right button likes a drink profile, which allows you to watch a video on preparing the drink and saves to your profile for later.
